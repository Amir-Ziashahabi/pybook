  • Multi Threading

    Running several threads is similar to running several different programs concurrently, but with two benefits: Multiple threads within a process share the same data space with the main thread and can therefore share information or communicate with each other more easily than if they were separate processes and Threads sometimes called light-weight processes and they do not require much memory overhead; they care cheaper than processes.
